Ingredients

  • 1 cup (225g) cottage cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ tsp garlic powder (optional)
  • ½ tsp Italian seasoning (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  2. 2Blend cottage cheese + eggs until smooth.
  3. 3Spread thin on lined baking sheet.
  4. 4Bake 28–35 min until golden.
  5. 5Cool 5 min, then peel off parchment.
  6. 6Top with avocado, veggies, or use as a wrap.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. 1

    Preheat and prepare

    Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper — do not skip the parchment, as the flatbread will stick to an unlined pan. Spray the parchment lightly with cooking spray for easy release. This is the only prep needed before you blend.

  2. 2

    Blend until completely smooth

    Add 1 cup of cottage cheese and 2 large eggs to a blender or food processor. Add garlic powder, Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper if using. Blend on high for 30–60 seconds until you have a completely smooth, lump-free batter. Scrape down the sides if needed and blend again. The smoother the batter, the more uniform the flatbread will be. A fork can work in a pinch, but a blender gives the best result.

  3. 3

    Spread thin and even — this step is critical

    Pour the batter onto the center of the prepared baking sheet. Using a spatula or the back of a spoon, spread it into a thin, even rectangle or oval — aim for no more than ¼ inch thick and roughly 10×7 inches. The thinner you spread it, the crispier the result. A thick pour leads to a soggy center. Take your time here to get it as even as possible from edge to edge.

  4. 4

    Bake until golden and firm

    Bake at 375°F for 28–35 minutes. The flatbread is done when the edges are clearly golden brown, the surface looks matte (not shiny), and the center springs back lightly when you press it with your finger. If the center still feels soft or wet, add 5 more minutes. Every oven runs slightly different, so check at 28 minutes. Do not rush this step — underbaked flatbread is the #1 reason people say it did not work.

  5. 5

    Cool before handling

    Remove from the oven and let the flatbread rest on the baking sheet for at least 5 minutes — it continues to firm up significantly as it cools. After 5 minutes, slide a thin spatula under the edge and carefully peel the flatbread off the parchment. Handle it gently; it is firm but not rigid like a cracker. It should feel like a thick crepe or thin pizza crust.

  6. 6

    Add your toppings and serve

    Use the flatbread immediately with your favorite toppings. For a high-protein meal: spread with hummus, layer on baby spinach, sliced cucumber, and smoked salmon. For a lunch wrap: add mashed avocado, cherry tomatoes, and a drizzle of hot sauce, then roll it up. For a pizza base: add marinara, mozzarella, and broil 2–3 minutes. Slice into pieces with a pizza cutter for easy serving.

Top Topping Ideas

🥑 Best Ways to Use Cottage Cheese Flatbread
  • Avocado toast style: Mash ½ avocado with lemon and salt, spread on flatbread, top with cherry tomatoes and red pepper flakes.
  • High protein wrap: Layer turkey slices, cucumber, spinach, and tzatziki sauce. Roll and slice in half.
  • Pizza base: Spread marinara, add shredded mozzarella and toppings. Broil 2–3 min.
  • Caprese style: Fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, basil, drizzle of olive oil and balsamic glaze.
  • Egg and veggie: Scrambled eggs, sautéed peppers, and hot sauce for a breakfast flatbread.
  • Hummus veggie: Generous hummus spread, roasted red peppers, olives, and cucumber — Mediterranean style.

Pro Tips

🏆 Tips for Perfect Cottage Cheese Flatbread
  • Spread it THIN — the single most important tip. Thick flatbread = soft and wet center.
  • Full-fat cottage cheese gives a richer flavor and slightly better texture than fat-free.
  • Don't open the oven early — steam builds up and helps the flatbread set. Check at 28 minutes only.
  • Let it cool! It goes from soft to firm in exactly 5 minutes as it cools. Do not panic if it feels soft when you pull it from the oven.
  • For extra flavor, add 1 tbsp nutritional yeast to the batter for a cheesy, nutty taste with no extra calories.
