Best Moist Banana Bread (Easy, 1 Bowl)

Ingredients

  • 3 very ripe bananas
  • ⅓ cup melted butter
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • ½ cup walnuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. 1Preheat Oven to 350°F. Grease loaf pan.
  2. 2Mash Bananas in a large bowl until smooth.
  3. 3Mix wet Stir in butter, sugar, egg, vanilla.
  4. 4Add dry Stir in baking soda, salt, cinnamon, flour.
  5. 5Fold Add mix-ins. Pour into pan.
  6. 6Bake 55–65 min until toothpick is clean.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. 1

    Preheat and prepare

    Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a standard 9x5 inch loaf pan with butter or non-stick cooking spray, then lightly dust with flour (or line with parchment paper for easy removal). A properly greased pan ensures the bread releases cleanly after baking.

  2. 2

    Mash the bananas

    Peel and place 3 very ripe bananas into a large mixing bowl. The riper the better — black-speckled bananas with soft skins are ideal and produce the sweetest, most flavorful bread. Mash thoroughly with a fork until mostly smooth with just a few small lumps remaining. You should have about 1½ cups of mashed banana.

  3. 3

    Mix in the wet ingredients

    To the mashed banana, stir in the melted butter until combined. Add the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract. Mix until just incorporated. Brown sugar can replace white sugar for a deeper, more caramel-like flavor — both work beautifully.

  4. 4

    Add the dry ingredients

    Sprinkle the baking soda, salt, and cinnamon directly into the bowl. Stir briefly to distribute, then add the flour all at once. Stir gently until just combined — stop as soon as you don't see dry flour streaks. Overmixing develops the gluten and makes the bread tough and dense instead of tender.

  5. 5

    Add mix-ins and pour

    If using, fold in ½ cup of chopped walnuts or chocolate chips (or both — it's a great combination). Pour the thick batter into your prepared loaf pan and smooth the top with a spatula. For an extra-pretty top, press a few banana slices lengthwise on top and sprinkle with a tiny pinch of sugar.

  6. 6

    Bake to golden perfection

    Bake at 350°F for 55–65 minutes. At the 55-minute mark, insert a toothpick or thin knife into the very center — it should come out clean or with a few moist crumbs (not wet batter). The top should be deeply golden brown and have a slight crack down the center. If the top is browning too fast, tent loosely with foil and continue baking. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes before turning out.

Pro Tips

🏆 Secrets to the Moistest Banana Bread
  • Use the RIPEST bananas possible — black spotted bananas are sweeter and contain more moisture than barely-ripe yellow ones.
  • Don't overmix after adding flour — stir only until combined. Overmixing = tough, dense bread.
  • The slight crack on top is a sign of a properly baked loaf — it means the outside set before the inside finished rising.
  • For extra moisture, substitute ¼ cup of the butter with sour cream or Greek yogurt.
  • Store wrapped at room temperature for up to 4 days, or freeze individual slices for up to 3 months.
