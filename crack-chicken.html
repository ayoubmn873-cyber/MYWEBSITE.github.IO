Crack Chicken Recipe — Creamy Ranch, Bacon, Cheddar (3 Ways)

About This Recipe

Crack chicken earned its name because it is genuinely impossible to stop eating. Slow-cooked chicken breasts shredded into a rich, creamy mixture of ranch-seasoned cream cheese, crispy bacon, and melted cheddar — it is the kind of dish that disappears from the table before you can get seconds. The recipe requires 5 minutes of prep, a slow cooker, and the patience to wait 6 hours. It can be served on toasted brioche buns as the best chicken sandwich you have ever made, over steamed rice, in lettuce wraps, stuffed in baked potatoes, or literally any other way you can think of. Three cooking methods are included: slow cooker, Instant Pot, and oven.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken breasts
  • 1 packet ranch seasoning
  • 8oz cream cheese, cubed
  • 6 strips bacon, cooked & crumbled
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar
  • Green onions to serve
  • Brioche buns for serving

Instructions

  1. 1Place chicken in slow cooker.
  2. 2Sprinkle ranch packet over chicken.
  3. 3Add cream cheese cubes on top.
  4. 4Cook LOW 6–8 hrs or HIGH 3–4 hrs.
  5. 5Shred chicken and stir with cream cheese.
  6. 6Add bacon + cheddar. Serve on buns.

3 Cooking Methods

⚡ Slow Cooker vs Instant Pot vs Oven
  • Slow Cooker (best): LOW 6–8 hrs or HIGH 3–4 hrs. Yields the most tender, juicy chicken. Best for mornings when you set it before work.
  • Instant Pot (fastest): Add ½ cup chicken broth. Cook High Pressure 15 min, natural release 10 min. Total time: 35 minutes. Nearly identical result.
  • Oven: Place all ingredients in a covered Dutch oven. Bake at 325°F for 2–2.5 hours until chicken shreds easily. Shred and stir in the cream cheese and bacon.
